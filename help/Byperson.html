By person

Search the database ››

Purpose

find datasets associated with a person.

Examples
  • datasets where Grimm, Eric Christopher is the Principal Investigator (PI)
  • datasets published by author Graham, Russell W.


  1. In the Search window, click on the Person button.
  2. Select the Person type to identify the person's relationship to the dataset(s).
  3. In the Person text box, type a the first few letters a person's family name to bring up a list of suggestions, then select one.
  4. Click the Search button or press ENTER on your keyboard to run the query.
