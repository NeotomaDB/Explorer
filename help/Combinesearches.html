Combine searches

Work with search results ››

Sometimes it is useful to combine searches for interpretation or presentation purposes. For example, you may want to find locations where two taxa co-occur, or where a taxon occurs in all of many time periods. Neotoma Explorer enables you to combine as many searches as you wish to create new map layers of interest.



  1. Click the button to open the Current Searches list.
  2. Check the boxes next to the searches you wish to combine, then click the Combine button.
  3. Type a name for your search.
  4. Select the combination logic:
    • AND to intersect search results
    • OR to union them
  1. Click OK to complete the combine or Back to cancel.
  2. If the combine operation yields at least one result record, the new search will be added to your list; otherwise, you will receive a notification in the Messages slider that no results were produced.
