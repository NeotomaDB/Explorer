Neotoma Explorer is an interactive mapping application that provides researchers, educators, and students with access to over 40,000 paleoecology datasets across 19,000+ geographic sites. Users can discover data through searches on spatial, temporal, and metadata criteria and explore data and information in a variety of formats (e.g. maps, graphs, exports, DOI landing pages). For more information, view the documentation.
